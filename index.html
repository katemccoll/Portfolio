Kate McColl

Hello! I am Kate, a Kiwi that is currently living over in Melbourne. Since completing my Fine Arts degree at the University of Canterbury in 2016, I have been working in an Australian art and gift shop. While I enjoy the personal aspect of my job, it drains a lot of my artistic ambition, so when I come home I often want to get away from art. A career as a web developer would be great because I'd be able to focus on solving interesting problems during the day, while still having the energy and desire to create my art outside of work.


Along with being creative, I am bubbly and known to always have a big smile on my face. I am dedicated and love looking at all the fine details, especially when it comes to puzzles and drawing realistic portraits. I love getting fully invested in something that I'm passionate about, and I find it incredibly rewarding when the hard works pays off.


I am currently enrolled at Monash University Coding Bootcamp, very intense and fast pace but enjoying learning and thinking about computers in a different way. I have really enjoyed creating websites from scratch and looking for the small details to make the page better. I love that I can bring my creative side to each of my assignments and using my knowledge to build a site like a jigsaw puzzle. I hope by the end of this course, I will have the confidence to build and create innovate websites and bring my art skills into the digital art world.
